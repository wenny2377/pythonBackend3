── For thesis (copy-paste) ──────────────────────────────────
To evaluate the RAG memory system's ability to resolve fuzzy
user requests, 12 fixed natural language queries were issued
to the InteractionEngine after Experiment 3 had populated
the behavioral memory. Queries ranged from direct expressions
('我口渴了') to abstract emotional states ('我有點累'),
spanning three semantic distance levels from the stored
behavioral memory.
The system returned a valid navigation target in 12 of
12 queries (100%), and produced a semantically
correct response in 8 of 12 cases
(overall accuracy = 67%), confirming that FAISS-based
semantic retrieval can bridge the vocabulary gap between
user expressions and stored behavioral observations.
